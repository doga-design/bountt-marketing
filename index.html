Bountt — The Free Expense Splitting App Built for Real Life

Bountt is a free expense splitting app that helps groups, roommates, friends, and coworkers track and divide shared costs without spreadsheets, awkward conversations, or constant payment reminders.

Split Bills With Roommates

Tired of being the one who covers rent, hydro, wifi, and groceries every month? Bountt makes it easy to track shared household expenses with your roommates and figure out who owes what — without the tension. Log any bill in seconds, assign who split it, and Bountt calculates the balance automatically.

Group Expense Tracking for Friends and Travel

Going on a group trip? Splitting dinner with friends? Bountt tracks every shared cost across the group and suggests the simplest way to settle up — minimizing the number of transactions between people so nobody ends up with six separate Venmo requests.

A Splitwise Alternative That Doesn't Feel Like a Bill Collector

Looking for a Splitwise alternative? Bountt was built to feel less like debt management and more like a tool among friends. No aggressive reminders, no awkward debt screens — just a clear picture of where things stand, when you want to look.

How to Split Shared Expenses With Your Group

With Bountt: one person logs the expense, tags who was involved, and the app handles the rest. Everyone can see the running tally in a shared feed. Settle up whenever makes sense — not on the app's schedule.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bountt free?
Yes. Bountt is completely free. No credit card, no premium tier, no ads.
How is Bountt different from Splitwise?
Bountt is designed around the way close groups actually split costs — casually, without debt framing or nagging. It's built for roommates and friend groups, not corporate expense reports.
Can I use Bountt to split rent and utilities with roommates?
Yes. Bountt handles recurring bills like rent, hydro, wifi, and groceries alongside one-off expenses like takeout or household supplies.
Does Bountt work for group trips?
Yes. Log any shared cost during a trip — accommodation, transport, food, activities — and Bountt calculates everyone's share and suggests the simplest way to settle when you're back.
